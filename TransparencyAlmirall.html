The challenge

I was asked to improve the design of the "Transparency" page with a simple, user friendly and responsive solution.

Link to website: Almirall.com/transparency .

The "Transparency" page presented a static non-responsive table with the complete list of countries and their corresponding methodological notes and reports.


To find the information of interest the user would have to search for the country and its reports throughout the table.

_________ Design

To reduce the noise and provide a more usable table of content I designed a filter so to help the user to complete easily his task both from desktop and mobile devices
