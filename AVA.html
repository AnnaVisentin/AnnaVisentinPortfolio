AVA

How to improve the Barcelona's public safety

Overview

Currently, Barcelona is facing an increase in crime. According to official data from the Ministry of Security, Barcelona in 2019 recorded more than 236.771 criminal infractions, 4.96% more than in the first half of 2018.

The main goal for this project is to propose a real improvement of the citizen's safety discovering what added value we should bring to the to the law enforcement or the city making Barcelona a reference and example to follow, at the height of the safest cities in the world..

The Problem

Citizens are increasingly frightened, they have a sense of insecurity associated with the perception of impunity towards repeat offenders.

The Solution

AVA (Asistencia Vigilancia Aerea) is a video surveillance tool that aims to help first responder supplying greater information at every step of emergency response.

_________ Kick off

We started to study the briefing provided to have an overall idea of what is the problem.
With the creation of an affinity map, we asked ourselves questions addressed to the three different actors: the client, the user (all citizens including tourists) and competitors.

_________ Research : Benchmark

We started by analyzing the competitors (safest cities) that ranked among the top 30 in the official data of SCI2019. We decided to take into account the cultural aspect, focusing our attention only to the eurpean cities. I also looked at multiple newspaper articles and police statistics.
Even though Barcelona ranks 19th in the general safest cities world ranking, the crime has dramatically increased, alarming also foreing governaments.

_________ Research : Survey & Interview

With the aim to understand what Barcelona's citizens think about the current public safety situation, we created an online survey to get information about user behaviour, experiences and thought.
After one day 158 people toke the survey giving us the following results:

Furthermore, to build empathy with our users we interviewed two sitizen and a police agent to get feedback from both parties about the current safety situation

Main citizen's frustrations:

  • Not being able to be relaxed when walking through the streets and public transports

  • The poor, long, unreliable reporting process

Main police officer frustrations:

  • Receiving frivolous reports, for which there is no solution

  • The negative opinion citizens have towards the security forces

_________ Define : Persona & User journey

To foster our user-centred design I analized all data collected to create two Personas and their journey to better visualize a typical day of our user and see what problems we could help to solve or what part of the journey we could improve.

Insights

Being able to walk in the streets especially at night, and taking public transport fearless of being robbed

Knowing that there is more surveillance, especially in some areas. Knowing that police is doing their best to keep the city safe

Have a reporting process faster and efficient. Knowing that all information reported will help to reduce crime.

_________ Define : Service Blueprint

Speaking of citizen security, we realized that a pain point is precisely the police station where to report a crime. This has led us to define and analyze a Service Blueprint of the reporting process at the police station.
Lack of organization and short of staff seems to be the main issues especially for those police stations located in the hot areas of the city being not able to cope quickly with the needs of those who enter to report.

_________ Ideation

Improving the reporting process within a police station, wouldn't change the feeling of fear of the citizen, but, improving the tools of those who should guarantee citizen security could probably have a positive consequence for everybody.
After a few ideations exercise, with a "Smart city"concept in mind, and taking into account the new technologies, we ended up with with the ideation of AVA.

Sensors, drones connected to a terminal in the patrol will allow law enforcement to see in real-time what is happening and in case of emergency, call reinforcements, ambulance or firefighter. Data logging with the drone camera enables low enforcement to start an investigation eventually.

_________ Testing

Being unable to test AVA with law enforcement agents, we decided to ask directly to citizens for their opinion. The test conducted was laboratory-based.
To help our users to imagine where and how AVA would be used, we recreated a car. The interviewer is the driver, while the user is the co-pilot.

We have carried out 4 tests, and all users considered the service useful. They found the interface easy and simple to use and the tool as something that can responds to the need for more control and timely intervention in case of an emergency.

_________ Conclusions
  • Thanks to the user's feedback we made some improvements to our prototype such as creating a clearer home menu with a description of the icons. Other changes have been made to the icons used in the maps.
  • Thanks to a deep user research and the Service Design process, we came up with a solution that can help to solve both citizens and client needs
  • As a citizen myself, concerned for my safety, I would be happy to know that I can walk through the same areas and alleys as I used to years ago. I would also like to know that those who are in charge of taking care of public safety in the city where I live, have the motivation and the necessary tools to complete their duty
